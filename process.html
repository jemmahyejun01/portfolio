Original Reference

In my third year of undergraduate studies, I have developed a portfolio to apply for jobs and internships. I referenced this portfolio as the foundation for the website, duplicating some of the layout and content, while updating some information and adding more data.

Colour Palette

According to Sensational Color, green represents balance, growth and learning. Green is also used in design to foster creativity and productivity. Based on these information, I wanted to portray my digital portfolio as a peaceful yet youthful vibrance. Some green colors were too harsh on the eyes so I picked more toned-down pastel colors.

Colour palette created with Coolors

Typography

Using Font Joy Website, I was able to experiment with different font styles and combninations. I wanted the typography to be modern yet not too rigid or fancy. Making sure the fonts for headings were not too drastically different from the body texts but still standing out was also an important factor. Therefore, I have decided on the three fonts: Jaldi, Shanti, and Voces.

Jaldi is perfect for big titles that grabs viewers' attention right away and was used for heading 1, 2 and 3. Smaller titles like heading 4 and 5 that needed to be differentiated from the body text used the Shanti font. The Voces font for the body text overall gave a clean look of the website.
